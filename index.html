MeznSat

UAE’s First 3U CubeSat for Monitoring Greenhouse Gases


OBJECTIVE

To design, build and test a 3U CubeSat for scientific applications by students at UAE educational institutions

To monitor Methane and Carbon Dioxide levels in the atmosphere over UAE 



ORBIT

The lifetime of the mission is three years

Minimum orbit of 450km

Conceptual mission is a SSO orbit with the altitude of 450-600km and inclination more than 90 degrees
